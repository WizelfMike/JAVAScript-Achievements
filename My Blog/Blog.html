Inside the Study

When you walk into the sudy, the first thing you'll notice is that when you look up there is no roof. Only neverending bookcases. The study itself has a very special property, When the door is shut time stops for everywhere except the study. This means you could read whatever you want for hours and not a second will go by. Aswel as that, all human needs become irrelivant. No need to sleep, eat, drink, you won't even age.
